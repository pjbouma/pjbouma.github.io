Tarak

Information

  • Tarak is a humanoid living on [Dragon's rest]

  • A gold hangman's noose is worked into the design of one of his visible tattoos. This noose is a symbol associated with the Gilded Gallows, a thieves' guild that operates in a country far to the southeast called Elturgard.

  • Visits the sea caves on the south side of the island often to acquire heart cap mushrooms from the myconids. He uses those to make potions of healing
